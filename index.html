TOP NYC EVENTS IN MARCH 2020

If you are visiting or moving to NYC, you should know the upcoming months are full of surprises. Beginning with March and the warmer weather that follows, you can start planning a few events that you should visit. Not only that you’ll finally put away your coats, hats, and scarfs, but you’ll also have a ton of fun. Of course, if your schedule allows it. Therefore, we bring you a list of a few notable NYC events in March so you can prepare on time.

Let us begin with one of the most popular NYC events in March. International Women’s Day

Like all over the world, you can find tons of activities on the 8th of March. It is an International Women’s Day and all activities are organized to honor the women of the world. There are a lot of free runs, workouts, special deals, and amazing offers. Keep in mind that as a male you can participate, but all those amazing deals are for women only. On this day, the most popular running race is organized. Stretching from Brooklyn over the Manhattan bridge finishing at the Central Park.

This is your day ladies! Enjoy and have a wonderful time.

If you are interested in this kind of activity, be there at 7:30 am in Prospect Park, Brooklyn. If you are new to the city and just moved in, this would be the perfect event to de-stress after a move in NYC. Also, you can visit a local mall to snatch one of the amazing deals or visit your favorite restaurant. They for sure have something waiting for you.

Women’s History Month

A whole month is dedicated to the better half. A smaller version of March the 8th but stretched throughout the month. It is amazing what these strong women organized for the beauties of the world. NYC has to offer many amazing events such as an all-female jazz festival, female stand up, blow dry bars, movie screenings, and other panels. Also, you should consider assisting in volunteering at women’s shelters and contribute to different levels. To sum it up, there are countless incredible NYC events in March. Every day in March can be a party if you wish so.

Unavoidable NYC events in March related to forever famous St. Patrick

On the 17th of March, we have St. Patrick’s Day where everyone in NYC becomes an Irish. This event is so famous that it attracts around 750.000 people annually. Irish pubs all over the city are fired up and you can hear people cheering and singing from a mile away. A jolly spirit and the beer of the highest quality is something you can’t ignore. Therefore, if you wish to participate prepare on time. The event begins at 11 AM on 44th Street and 5th Avenue.

Put on your green shirt and let the Irish song lift you up. After such an event you’ll wish to become a part of the city. The Armory Show

One of NYC’s most famous art shows will take place on 5-8 March. Yes, we are talking about The Armory Show. For four days you’ll be able to see some of the most amazing arts from prestigious art galleries and museums such as the Metropolitan Museum of Art. And all in one place, at Piers 94 and 92. It is a platform that speaks for 21st-century visual art. A leader among the NYC events in March and something you shouldn’t miss. If you are an artistic soul and you wish to witness a display of innovative modern art, be sure to make a visit. It is one of the biggest art fairs worldwide and since it’s foundation 25 years ago, it attracts around 65k visitors annually. It is the modern art frontier and you should be part of it.

The Orchid Show

Along came the Spring and we are all part of it. The Orchid Show is an ode to Spring and a way to honor it in NYC. You can attend this event any day from March through April at The New York Botanical Gardens. Witness an exhibit of a thousand different blossoming orchids as a marvel of nature. Take a look through the kaleidoscope prism of colors in yet another cycle of rebirth.

Visit The Orchid Show. It is heaven on earth!
